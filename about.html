About US

Namchow (Thailand) Ltd. was established in 1991 as a manufacturer and marketeer of Rice Crackers (Senbei & Usuyaki), Rice Snacks and Instant Noodles for the world - wide market. This is the first overseas investment made by the Namchow Group in Taiwan.

With the combination of Thai resources, new advanced technology, product development, and our management skills; together with the full support from our parent company in Taiwan, our business development has been very successful in the world markets as well as the business domestically.

Over the past year, we have already developed our own business model for business running as below:

  • Customer relationship
  • Cooperative R & D
  • Produce to order
  • Sustainable cost cycle

From which, it also leads us to be the one of leading Rice Crackers, Rice Snacks and Instant Noodles supplier in the world markets.

Since we realized quality and hygiene were the key issues of the highest product standards that we could offer to the international market, we therefore dedicated our greatest efforts in the past years to obtaining HACCP, GMP, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, BRC, IFS, Kosher, Halal certifications. This has been helpful to improve and control our production process to ensure our products' quality for customers. Regarding our business; we run 'own Brand' such as 'Bin Bin' & 'Shinmai' for rice products and 'Little Cook' & 'Mee Jang' for instant noodles in the world overseas.

Asian market and domestic as well - our export market expect overseas and Asian market now is focused on the mainstream market, which is under our own brand "Rice Cracks and Rice Chips" for rice products. Except own brand business, we also welcome for the 'Private Label' or 'House Brand' for any interested parties. Every year, we participate in at least 4 of the biggest world food exhibitions. After several years business development, we have already established our business relationship over 75 countries and territories.

  • To be The Top Noodles manufacturer in the niche world market
  • To fit in the world-class management to achieve No.1 player
